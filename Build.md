How to Use T.A.P.!

Step 1: Open The .SB3 in Turbowarp.

Step 2: Add Assets.

Substep 1: Add All your sprites into Sprite1.

Substep 2: Add Any Voice Lines to Sprite1's Audio. Anything not assigned a line will be spoken with TTS.


Step 3: Making Name tags.
